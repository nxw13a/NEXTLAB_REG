    The NEXT Lab has a team of innovative researchers at Abilene Christian University focused on providing global solutions to the world's most critical needs through the development of molten salt reactors. We strive for excellence and work hard everyday to provide solutions to the need for energy, water and medical isotopes. We invite you to join our team!

    NEXTLAB Internships

    The mission of ACU’s NEXT Lab is to provide global solutions to the world’s need for energy, water and medical isotopes by advancing the technology of molten salt reactors while educating future leaders in nuclear science and engineering.

    NEXT hires students three times a year: for the fall and spring semesters, where students can work up to 25 hours a week, and in the summer, hwere studenst work as full-time NEXT interns. Our hiring cycles trypically follow this basic shedule:

    • For fall work, apply by early Septermber
    • For spring work, apply by mid-November
    • For summer work, apply by mid-February

    We are always looking for the best and the brightest students, especially those with engineering, physics, chemistry, math, and computer science majors. You are invited to apply to work with us at any time, and your application will be kept on file until the next hiring cycle.
